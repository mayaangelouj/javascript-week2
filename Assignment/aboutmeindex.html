As the owner of Milk Cafe and art studio, Jasmine welcomes you to a space where passion and creativity collide in a harmonious blend. Drawing inspiration from her love of both coffee and art, Jasmine has crafted a haven that celebrates the beauty of artistic expression and the joy of savoring a perfectly crafted cup of coffee. With a background in fine arts and a deep appreciation for the ritual of coffee brewing, She has curated a space where artistry takes center stage, whether through the striking paintings adorning the walls or the meticulous attention to detail in every espresso shot poured. Here, every latte is a canvas, every sip a symphony of flavors waiting to be savored. She eagerly invites you to join her on a journey of exploration and discovery, where the boundaries between art and coffee blur, and inspiration flows freely. Whether you're an artist seeking a tranquil space to create or a coffee enthusiast looking to immerse yourself in a world of flavor, this coffee shop and art studio is a reflection of her commitment to fostering a sense of community, creativity, and connection. So come, sip on a masterpiece and let your imagination soar as you bask in the warm embrace of art and coffee.
